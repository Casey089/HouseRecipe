Garlic Salmon with Lemon Butter
a recipe by Erin Clarke
Ingredients:
. Butter (3 moderate slices)
. Garlic (1 cup)
. Lemon Zest (1 cup)
. Parsley (2 pinches)
. Pepper (1-2 teaspoons)
. Salmon
. Green Beans
. Tomatoes
. Olive Oil
. Lemon Juice
Steps:
1: Mix the Garlic/Butter/Lemon Zest/Parsley into a paste
2: Spread the paste vertically along the salmon and place on baking sheet
3: Mix Olive Oil/Tomatoes/Green Beans/Salt/Pepper in a bowl, place around the salmon
4: Douse the dish in lemon juice, place lemon wheels around the dish
5: Bake 16-20 Minutes, Serve!
